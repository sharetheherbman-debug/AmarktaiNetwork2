📅 Our Story

Built in South Africa.
Built for the World.

2023 · The Idea

A Gap in the Market

Founder recognises that African entrepreneurs lack the AI infrastructure that Silicon Valley companies take for granted. The idea for Amarktai Network is born.

2024 · First Products

Amarktai Crypto & EquiProfile Launch

The first two apps go live. Amarktai Crypto becomes invite-only within weeks due to demand. EquiProfile gains 3,000+ users in the first month.

2025 · The Ecosystem

8 More Apps in Development

The Amarktai Network vision crystallises. 8 additional AI applications in active development across property, forex, security, jobs, and community verticals.

2026 · Now

The Full Network Goes Live

All 10 apps on a unified platform. 12,000+ users. 247 AI agents running 24/7. The most advanced AI ecosystem built in Africa — live and growing.
